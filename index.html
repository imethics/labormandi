Frequently Asked Questions

How does the app help in managing daily wage workers?
The app digitizes everything from onboarding to daily attendance and payments, streamlining operations and removing manual paperwork completely.
Is the attendance tracking feature GPS-based?
Yes, it uses advanced geofencing to ensure workers can only punch in when they are physically present at the designated work site.
How secure is the payment processing within the app?
We use bank-grade encryption and native UPI integrations to ensure 100% secure, direct-to-bank automated transfers.

Absolutely. You can generate, share, and digitally sign legally compliant contracts directly from your mobile device.
